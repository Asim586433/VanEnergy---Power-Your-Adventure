Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our energy solutions.

How long does installation take? +

Most installations are completed within 1-2 days, depending on the complexity of your system and vehicle setup.

What maintenance is required? +

Our systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend periodic cleaning of solar panels and an annual system check to ensure optimal performance.

Do you offer financing? +

Yes, we offer several financing options to make our energy solutions accessible. Contact us for details on current financing programs.

What is the warranty on your systems? +

Warranties vary by package: Essential (1 year), Adventure (3 years), Expedition (5 years). All components also carry manufacturer warranties.

Can I expand my system later? +

Yes, all our systems are designed with expansion in mind. We can easily add more solar panels or batteries as your needs grow.

About VanEnergy

Our Story

VanEnergy was founded by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who struggled with unreliable power during their van life adventures. Frustrated with existing solutions, we set out to create energy systems specifically designed for life on the road.

Today, we've helped thousands of van lifers, RV travelers, and outdoor enthusiasts power their adventures with reliable, efficient energy solutions.

Our Mission

To enable freedom and adventure through reliable, sustainable energy solutions that empower people to live and work wherever their journeys take them.

Our Values

Sustainability

Reliability

Innovation
